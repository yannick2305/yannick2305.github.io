About Me

Short CV

I am a PhD student in computational and applied mathematics at the University of Oslo under the supervision of Prof. Erik Orvehed Hiltunen. Previously I was a master student at ETH Zurich, working under the supervision of Prof. Habib Ammari.

My recent work involves studying the localisation phenomenon in metamaterials and obtaining quantitative localisation strength using the complex band structure.

Outside of academia, I enjoy nature.
