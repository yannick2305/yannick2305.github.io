About Me

Short CV

I am a mathematics student currently pursuing my studies at ETH Zurich. My academic interests lie in mathematical physics, particularly in wave propagation, scattering theory, and spectral analysis.

My recent work involves using layer potential techniques to solve the Helmholtz scattering problem in periodic media. In addition, I have explored topics related to Toeplitz operators and their applications in spectral theory.

Outside of academia, I enjoy coding, collaborating on open-source projects, and deepening my understanding of the mathematical foundations behind physical phenomena.
