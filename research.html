Research

Current Research Interests

My research focuses on the propagation of waves in periodic and quasi-periodic structures, particularly in the context of metamaterials. These artificial media can exhibit exotic wave phenomena, including band gaps and strong localisation.

I investigate these behaviors using a combination of:

  • Layer potential techniques for boundary value problems in complex geometries.
  • Scattering theory and the Helmholtz equation.
  • Spectral theory and the analysis of Toeplitz operators.

Recent Topics

I am currently working on understanding localisation effects in metamaterials, with an emphasis on quantifying them using the complex band structure. These techniques help assess how confined waves are within the material and how energy can be trapped or redirected.

My approach is analytical, but also supported by computational methods where appropriate.
